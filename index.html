MEN'S BEST COMPANION

Dogs are often considered man's best friend because of their loyal and affectionate nature. They have been domesticated for thousands of years and have evolved to be highly attuned to human emotions and behavior. Dogs are social animals that thrive on companionship and have a natural desire to please their owners. They are able to provide a variety of benefits to their human companions, including companionship, protection, and even therapeutic support. The close bond between dogs and humans has been shown to have numerous positive effects on both mental and physical health, making them invaluable companions and loyal friends.

HUSKY

A breed's level of tolerance and patience with childrens' behavior, and overall family-friendly nature. Dogs should always be supervised around young children, or children of any age who have little exposure to dogs.

CHOW CHOW

Chows make good family pets but aren't known for their playfulness or patience so they are better suited to families without boisterous younger children. As well as being physically strong, Chow Chows can be quite strong-willed and stubborn so they are not the best choice for novice owners.

GOLDEN RETRIEVER

Goldens are outgoing, trustworthy, and eager-to-please family dogs, and relatively easy to train. They take a joyous and playful approach to life and maintain this puppyish behavior into adulthood. These energetic, powerful gundogs enjoy outdoor play

DASHSHUND

Despite their size, dachshunds are known for their courageous nature and will take on animals much larger than themselves. Some may be aggressive toward strangers and other dogs. As family dogs, dachshunds are loyal companions and good watchdogs. They are good with children if treated well.

CHUA CHUA

Chihuahuas are loving and loyal and make good pets for first-time owners. These little dogs have big personalities and range from feisty and outgoing to shy and timid. Intelligent and fiercely loyal to the people they love, Chihuahuas will happily accompany their owners anywhere and everywhere.

SHIH TZU

The Shih Tzu is known to be especially affectionate with children. As a small dog bred to spend most of their day inside royal palaces, they make a great pet if you live in an apartment or lack a big backyard.

FRIENDSHIP

Dogs are known for their friendly and sociable nature, which has been developed through thousands of years of domestication and breeding. Through the process of domestication, dogs have evolved to be highly attuned to human emotions and behavior, allowing them to understand and respond to human social cues. They are social animals that have a natural desire to form close bonds with other living beings, including humans. Additionally, dogs are highly trainable and have been bred for specific traits, such as obedience and loyalty, which make them excellent companions and friends. Overall, the combination of their sociability, trainability, and loyalty has made dogs one of the most beloved and cherished animals for companionship and friendship.
